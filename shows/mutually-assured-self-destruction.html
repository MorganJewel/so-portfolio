Mutually Assured Self-Destruction: i wanna feel pretty

Written by Beddie Slatkoff & Sofi Olona

Mutually Assured Self-Destruction is a camp, surrealist play exploring the psychological effects of what happens when we distort ourselves for a North Star. Contestants enter the Miss Sexy but You Would Still Be Happy to Have Her be the Mother of Your Children, Illinois pageant only to each fall into their own internal fantasies and nightmares. How bad do you want it? What do we lose on the journey?

This show was a sophomore year project pitched by Beddie Slatkoff and Sofi Olona.
