Chicago Pope Weighs Heavy on my Heart

Devised by Cecil Bell, Harrison Benjamin, Meredith Hope Blackcreek, C. Ryan Holmes, & Grace Sims — Directed by Sofi Olona

Chicago Pope Weighs Heavy on my Heart is an adaptation of a Catholic mass deconstructed with love by a group of queer individuals. This production was a fundraiser for The Trevor Project. "Did we leave or were we forced out?" "Does my butt look good in this heaven?"

Using music, puppetry, poetry, prayer, and dance we created the mass we needed to heal old wounds.

This show was my junior year project, pitched and selected by faculty after I freaked out about the election of Pope Leo XIV.
