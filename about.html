I'm a Mexican-American theatrical artist born and raised in Los Angeles, based in Chicago. And guys...I'm really good.

Currently, I am in my fourth year at The Theatre School at DePaul University getting a BFA in Theatre Arts with a Concentration in Directing. Upon my studies I discovered a deep love for experimental, devised and new work. I thrive in the "maybe."

As the daughter of two psychologists from East LA, I see being an artist as a privilege that will not be left to waste. I love creating work that leans towards healing. How can we create artistic practices and processes that support artists' well beings? How can the shows we present open a dialogue with the self? I strive to create rehearsal rooms and shows that support play, intellectual rigor and do not destroy artists' mental health.

Across my various theatrical disciplines you might have met me working at The Goodman, Lookingglass, The Neofuturists, TimeLine Theatre Company, and Free Street Theater...and I would love to work with you!

When I'm not in a rehearsal room, you will probably find me hanging out with AC (my cat), playing with my friends, at a thrift shop, or rewatching Sex and the City (I'm a Miranda and Carrie combo).
